About Us – The Keepers

We are The Keepers, a team of 6th-grade students proudly taking part in the Digitobia Competition.

Our team members are Malak, Ziad, Anas, and Hana. We are not classmates from the same school — we come from different schools, but we joined together because we share a common vision: to protect our environment, our heritage, and our future.

We chose the name The Keepers because we see ourselves as guardians of civilization. Living in Alexandria, we are inspired by its rich history and culture; however, we also recognize the challenges posed by climate change, flooding, and plastic pollution.

Through our project Samir, we want to prove that young people can create real solutions using technology solutions that help families, schools, and communities take action for a sustainable future.
