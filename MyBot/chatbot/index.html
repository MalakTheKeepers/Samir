Who We Are

Samir is a collective of environmental scientists, data engineers, and urban planners dedicated to building the sustainable cities of the future. We believe that technology, specifically AI and machine learning, is the most powerful tool we have to address the climate crisis at a local level.

Our work is driven by a commitment to transparency, community involvement, and impactful, measurable results. We partner with city governments and local communities to ensure our solutions are practical and scalable.
